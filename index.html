Can I use AI-generated images commercially?

Images generated by AI tools generally fall into a gray area regarding copyright. For personal projects, blogs, and social media, AI images are widely used. For commercial products, check with a legal professional about your specific use case. We recommend reading our terms of service for details.

What makes a good AI image prompt?

The best prompts are specific and descriptive. Include the subject, style (photorealistic, watercolor, digital art), lighting, mood, and composition details. For example, instead of 'a dog,' try 'a golden retriever sitting in autumn leaves, warm sunlight, shallow depth of field, professional photography.' Check our prompt tips page for more guidance.

How long does it take to generate an image?
