Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DreamForge AI.

Is DreamForge AI really free? ▼
Yes, DreamForge AI is 100% free. You get 3 image creations per session. After that, watch a short ad to unlock 3 more. There is no paid tier, no subscription, and no credit card required — ever. We're supported by ads so you never have to pay.
Do I need to create an account or sign up? ▼
No. DreamForge AI requires absolutely no signup. No email, no password, no account. Just open the page and start typing your prompt. Your images are generated instantly in your browser.
What art styles are available? ▼
We offer 8 distinct styles: Photorealistic (camera-quality), Digital Art (vibrant concept art), Oil Painting (classical brushstrokes), Anime (Japanese animation), Watercolor (soft artistic washes), 3D Render (CGI quality), Pixel Art (retro game style), and Sketch (hand-drawn linework). Plus an Auto mode that lets the AI choose. See our full styles guide →
What image sizes can I create? ▼
DreamForge AI supports 4 sizes: Square (1:1) at 1024x1024 — great for social media and profiles; Landscape (4:3) at 1024x768 — perfect for blog headers; Portrait (3:4) at 768x1024 — ideal for phone wallpapers and posters; Wide (16:9) at 1280x720 — perfect for YouTube thumbnails and presentations.
How long does image generation take? ▼
Most images are generated in 10-30 seconds. Complex prompts with lots of detail may take slightly longer. The generation happens on remote AI servers, so your device speed doesn't matter — it works great even on phones.
Can I use the images commercially? ▼
AI-generated images exist in an evolving legal landscape. DreamForge AI places no restrictions on how you use your created images. However, we recommend consulting a legal professional if you plan to use them in commercial products, advertisements, or publications. For personal use, social media, blogs, and inspiration — go wild!
How many images can I create? ▼
You start with 3 free creations per session. After using all 3, you can watch a short ad to unlock 3 more. There's no daily limit — you can keep watching ads for more creates as many times as you want.
How do I write better prompts? ▼
The key is being specific. Instead of "a cat," try "a fluffy orange tabby cat sitting on a windowsill, looking out at rain, warm lighting." Add details about lighting, mood, camera angle, and colors. We wrote a full guide on this: Read our prompt tips guide →
Does DreamForge AI store my images? ▼
No. Your images are generated in real-time and displayed in your browser. We do not store, save, or have access to any images you create. If you want to keep an image, download it before leaving the page.
What AI model powers DreamForge? ▼
DreamForge AI uses advanced open-source image generation models. The specific model may be updated over time to ensure you always get the best possible results.
Does it work on mobile phones? ▼
Yes! DreamForge AI is fully responsive and works on any device — iPhone, Android, tablet, or desktop. The generation happens on remote servers, so it works just as fast on a phone as on a powerful computer.
How is this different from Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion? ▼
The biggest difference is that DreamForge AI is completely free with no signup. Midjourney costs $10-60/month and requires a Discord account. DALL-E charges per image through ChatGPT Plus ($20/month). Stable Diffusion is free but requires a powerful GPU and technical setup. DreamForge gives you instant access to AI art generation right in your browser.
